Why This Platform Exists

Under the STOCK Act of 2012, U.S. members of Congress are legally required to disclose their stock transactions.

Capitol Trades Tracker makes this public data easier to explore by aggregating disclosures, applying AI-driven analysis, and presenting information in a clear, accessible format.

  • Aggregates official congressional trading disclosures
  • Highlights historical and behavioral patterns
  • Improves access to public transparency data
  • Operates independently of any government body
